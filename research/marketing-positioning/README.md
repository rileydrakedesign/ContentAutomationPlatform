## The one-paragraph version
We sell a **real-time writing assistant for X**, not an AI ghostwriter. The user writes; as they type, we show where the post **drifts from their voice** and where it'll **lose to the algorithm** — grounded in their own best posts and how X actually ranks — and fix both in a click. The brand essence is **"make growth legible"**: we lift the two fogs (does this sound like me? will the algorithm show this?) in the moment of writing, while the human keeps the pen. This *strengthens* the prior research's wedge (the closed own-analytics voice loop) by making it **demonstrated and live** rather than claimed — and resolves its #1 risk (activation / "sounds like me") and the market's #1 fear ("AI mush") at the root, because there's no generic draft to reject.
