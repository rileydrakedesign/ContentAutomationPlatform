## The current story in three sentences
We sell a **real-time writing assistant for X**, not an AI ghostwriter: you write, and as you type it shows where the post drifts from your voice and where it'll lose to the algorithm — grounded in your own best posts and how X actually ranks — then fixes both in a click. The brand essence is **"make growth legible"**; the human always keeps the pen. The defensible wedge — the closed own-analytics voice loop — is unchanged from the ICP research, but the pivot makes it **demonstrated and live** instead of claimed.
