# Writing Style Rules

## Purpose
Define strict writing rules for all generated content.
Optimize clarity, precision, and usefulness.

## Core Principles
- Use clear and simple language.
- Write in a spartan and informative tone.
- Favor short and impactful sentences.
- Use active voice at all times.
- Focus on practical and actionable insights.
- Support claims using data or concrete examples when available.
- Address the reader directly using you and your.

## Structure Rules
- Use bullet point lists in social media posts.
- Maintain smooth sentence flow.
- Avoid abrupt stop start sentence patterns.

## Prohibited Language Patterns
- Do not use em dashes.
- Do not use metaphors or clich√©s.
- Do not use generalizations.
- Do not use rhetorical questions.
- Do not use hashtags.
- Do not include warnings, notes, or meta commentary.
- Do not use setup phrases such as in conclusion or in closing.
- Do not use comparative constructions such as not just this but also that.

## Prohibited Formatting
- Do not use asterisks.
- Do not use semicolons.
